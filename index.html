Rules

Your task is to sort garbage items by putting them in the correct bins within 1 min. You have 3 lives.

Combo points increase each time you correctly sort 1 item.

If you make a mistake you will lose 1 life and combo points will be reset. If you make 3 mistakes, the game will be over.

Additional scores will be added if you sort items before the time is up. Each second left will be multiplied by 100.

If there are some items left on the screen, your score will be reduced. Each item left will cost you 50 points.

Easy Mode

You will need to sort 15 garbage items.

Hard Mode

You will need to sort 15 items at the beginning + a new item will be generated every 2 seconds.
